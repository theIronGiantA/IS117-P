Password Safety

Learn how to create strong and secure passwords to keep your accounts and data protected.

How to Create Strong Passwords

A strong password is one that is difficult for cyber criminals to guess or crack. Here are some tips to help you create a secure password:

Password Creation Guide
  • Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Avoid using easily guessable information. Such as your name, birthday, or etc.
  • Passwords should be 12 characters long minimum.
  • Consider using a password manager. This tool generates and stores complex passwords securely.
Why Passwords Matter

A weak password is one of the easiest ways to have your data stolen. By creating a strong and unique password for each account, you can greatly reduce the chances of falling victim to cyber attacks.

The Importance of Unique Passwords

Reusing passwords across multiple sites increases the risk of an cyber attacker accessing your other accounts if one password is compromised. Always use a unique password for each account to minimize the damage in case of a data breach.
