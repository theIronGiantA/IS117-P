Phishing Awareness

This guide will educate you on how to recognize and avoid phishing attacks.

What is Phishing?

Phishing is an online scam where hackers pretend to be real companies or people to steal your personal information. They try to get things like your usernames, passwords, or credit card numbers. These scams usually show up in fake emails, texts, or websites that look real but are made to trick you into giving away your info.

Common Phishing Techniques
  • Fake login pages that appear to be from trusted websites.
  • Email messages pretending to be from reputable companies, asking for personal details.
  • Tactics to create a sense of urgency or fear. For example an email that says 'Act now or your account will be locked".
How to Protect Yourself from Phishing
  • Always double-check the URL of websites you visit and ensure they are legitimate.
  • Don't click on suspicious email links or attachments from unknown sources.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for maximum security.
