Quick steps to stay safe online

Cybersecurity Checklist

  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Ensure software and apps are always up to date.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links or email attachments. Make sure a link is safe.
  • Keep backups of your data. Regulary update those backups.
  • Start using antivirus and firewall protection services.
  • If you ever use a personal accoint on a shared netowrk, make sure to sign out.
